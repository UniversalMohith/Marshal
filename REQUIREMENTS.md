# HACKATHON REQUIREMENTS & FACTS (build against these)

Microsoft **Agents League Hackathon** (Innovation Studio / AI Skills Fest).
Verified from the official rules on 2026-06-13. Re-check the live pages if in doubt.

## Key dates
| Milestone | When |
|---|---|
| Entry period | May 19 2026 12:00 AM PT -> **June 14 2026 11:59 PM PT** |
| Deadline in UK time | ~**08:00, June 15 2026 BST** |
| Winners announced | June 30 2026 |

No separate registration cutoff blocks submission; the entry period is the deadline.

## Eligibility
- 18 years or older.
- NOT a Microsoft employee / affiliate.
- Not void in Mohith's region (UK is fine).
- Mohith is an enrolled university **student** -> eligible for the **Top Student Award**
  (rides alongside whatever track he enters; winners must be enrolled students).

## Tracks (pick one; Reasoning is DECIDED)
| Track | Required tool |
|---|---|
| Creative Apps | GitHub Copilot |
| **Reasoning Agents (CHOSEN)** | **Microsoft Foundry (Azure AI Foundry)** |
| Enterprise Agents | M365 Agents Toolkit / Copilot Studio |

Microsoft tooling is effectively REQUIRED: a submission must include an architecture
diagram **showing use of the specified Microsoft tools**. A pure third-party build is
off-spec and loses the 20% relevance score.

## What every submission MUST include
1. A **public GitHub repository** with source code and a README.
2. A **project description** (features, functionality, the problem solved, the technologies).
3. A **demo video, 5 minutes maximum**, uploaded to YouTube or Vimeo (link in the submission).
4. An **architecture diagram** showing use of the specified Microsoft tools.
5. Team member info with **Microsoft Learn usernames** (if applicable).

## Entry limits
- Up to **3 submissions per challenge** and **9 total** per person.
- Solo plan: put everything into ONE strong entry rather than spreading thin.

## Judging rubric (the "90%" is everything except the community vote)
| Criterion | Weight | What it rewards |
|---|---|---|
| Accuracy & Relevance | 20% | Meets the challenge requirements |
| Reasoning & Multi-step Thinking | 20% | Clear problem-solving approach |
| Creativity & Originality | 15% | Novel ideas or unexpected execution |
| User Experience & Presentation | 15% | Clear, polished, demoable |
| Reliability & Safety | 20% | Solid patterns, avoids obvious pitfalls |
| Community vote | 10% | Discord poll |
